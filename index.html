ArcheOS

ArcheOS is the acronym of Archeological Operating System. It is a GNU/Linux live distribution built for archaeological aims.
ArcheOS is developed following the OpArc project guidelines and it is released by Arc-Team s.n.c. under the General Public License (GPL).

More info

Stable.

Build on top of debian, the most stable GNU/Linux distribution

All included

Archeos is shipped with a very large set of free software from photogrammetry to CAD, from mesh editing to GIS.
